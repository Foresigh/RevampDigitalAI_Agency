FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about how we work and what to expect.

What exactly is the free demo? +

We build a fully functional homepage redesign specifically for your business using your real name, logo, services, and colors. It's not a mockup or a screenshot. It's a live webpage you can click through, hosted temporarily so you can share it. We send you the link within 48 hours. No charge, no credit card required.

What if I don't move forward after the demo? +

No problem at all. We won't pressure you, chase you, or charge you anything. The demo is genuinely free. If it's not the right fit or the timing isn't right, you keep the demo and we part on good terms. That said most business owners who see their demo sign up on the spot.

Do I need to provide anything for the demo? +

Just your business name, industry, and any existing website or social media links you have. The more you give us, the more accurate the demo. But even if you have nothing we can work with just a name and a phone number.

How long does the full website take to launch? +

After you approve the demo and pay the one-time fee, we typically launch your full site in 5–10 business days. We'll need a few things from you: your logo (or we design one), photos (or we source them), and final copy review. We make the process as smooth and hands-off as possible.

Can I cancel the monthly plan anytime? +

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. You can cancel at the end of any billing month with 7 days' notice. We're confident you'll see enough value to stay but we'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.

How do you accept payment? +

All payments are processed securely through Stripe. We accept all major credit and debit cards. Monthly plans are billed automatically on the same date each month. You'll receive an invoice email for every payment.
