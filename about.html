Our Story

Built to Help the Businesses That Build Your Community

Revamp Digital AI was built on a simple frustration: great local businesses — the plumbers, barbers, roofers, and dentists that communities actually depend on — were getting left behind online. Their competitors with slicker websites and active social pages were winning, not because they were better, but because they looked better.

We created the free demo model specifically to remove every barrier to entry. No pitch decks, no discovery calls, no deposits. We just build you something that proves we can help — and let the work speak for itself.

Using AI-assisted design tools and a lean, scalable system, we can produce high-quality websites and social content faster and at a fraction of what traditional agencies charge — and pass those savings directly to our clients.

How We Operate

Our Daily Workflow

Behind the scenes, here's how we find clients, build demos, and deliver results every single day.

1. Find Leads

Identify local businesses with poor, outdated, or no website presence using Google Maps, Instagram, and industry directories.

2. Build the Demo

Using AI-assisted tools, we build a custom homepage specifically for that business — ready to share in under 48 hours.

3. Send Outreach

We reach out via email, Instagram DM, or website contact form — sharing the demo link as the opening message. No pitch needed.

4. Follow Up & Close

We follow up with interested prospects, answer questions, and close the website sale. Average close rate: 1 in 5 demos sent.

5. Launch & Deliver

We launch the full website, set up hosting, connect analytics, and onboard the client to the dashboard and monthly services.

6. Grow Every Month

Monthly social posts, video edits, SEO, and reporting keep the client growing. Recurring revenue compounds as we scale.
