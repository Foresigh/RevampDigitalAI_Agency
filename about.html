My Story

From Ethiopia to Self-Driving Vehicles — and Now This

My name is Feyisa Berisa. I was born in Ethiopia, earned a degree in Business & Leadership there, then moved to the United States to study Computer Engineering at Utah State University. I went on to earn my Master's in Electrical Engineering and eventually landed a role building software for self-driving vehicles — working with LiDAR, Radar, and robotic systems that push the edge of what technology can do.

But while I was spending my days writing code for autonomous machines, I kept noticing something that bothered me closer to home: local businesses — the barber who's been cutting hair for 20 years, the family-owned plumbing company, the dentist everyone loves — they were invisible online. Not because they weren't great at what they do, but because no one had built them something that showed it.

That's why I started Revamp Digital AI. I bring the same precision and systems thinking I use in software engineering to digital marketing — building websites, content systems, and growth strategies that actually work. And I do it at a price that makes sense for small business owners, not big-agency budgets.
